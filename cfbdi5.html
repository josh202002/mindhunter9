PRIDE BRINGS FALL

“Love is unconditional”, but according to society it has some conditions. Rasheed and Sanjana, were in deep love. But one thing, which came as an obstacle in their love, was society. Both of their families were against them due to the pressure of society. But both got married. Next day onwards, Rasheed’s father began to assault Sanjana and Rasheed. It went on for a few days. One day, Rasheed’s father was found hanged in his own house with something written on a letter.

Findings by Forensics

Several findings were found on the body and the investigation officers are still confused about the language scripts. They were sure about something was hidden in those scripts.
