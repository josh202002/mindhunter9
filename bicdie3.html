EVIL FOLLOWS LAZINESS

We always consider old people as a burden. When no one looks after them, Old Age Home becomes a shelter for them. In a small old age home, a new worker came as a caretaker. These people require an all-time assistance, but this caretaker didn’t care at all. Due to his laziness, a poor old lady was died because he didn’t deliver the medicine on time. But sometimes “laziness” can really kill you. The caretaker’s body was found on a wheel chair in a burnt condition.

Findings by Forensics

Several findings were found on the body and the investigation officers are still confused about the language scripts. They were sure about something was hidden in those scripts.
